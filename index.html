First Aid (فریاگوزاری سەرەتایی)

Xwendnga app is my latest project, I joined the Lozhic group to build that app as an iOS Developer.

CoronaTracker

CoronaTracker is a free, open-sourced, online app for tracking COVID-19 outbreak cases based on countries. All data of this app has provided by Microsoft's Bing and will be updated daily.
Apple won't approve any coronavirus-themed app created by individual developers, so I open-sourced this app and you can install on your physical phone and use it.
Open-sourced at GitHub.com.

DubDubDown

DubDubDown is my latest app, which lets you know exactly when the WWDC20 confrerence by Apple will be started. Plus, it sends a notification (If you click on the Remind Me button) when the conference is about to start.
This includes a watchOS app, as well as a macOS app.
Open-sourced at GitHub.com.
